Atomic Habits — Key Takeaways

#book #habits #productivity #growth
Success is the product of daily habits — not once-in-a-lifetime transformations. — James Clear

1. The Power of Tiny Changes

Small habits, repeated consistently, compound into remarkable results.

  • 1% better every day → 37x better in a year.
  • Focus on becoming 1% better, not achieving perfection.
Key Idea: You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems.

2.2 Make it Attractive
